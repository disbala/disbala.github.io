2Bit

Branding / Web Design 2019
Have you ever been annoyed of trying to find the retro game that you need, in the condition you want? Do you struggle with foreign sellers that try to rip you off? 2Bit Market just may be the solution. 2Bit is an exciting new platform to sell and buy retro video games. For the people, by the people.
RESEARCH & PROCESS
Research was done at the start of this project to highlight what the customers needed and what problems they experienced in their day-to-day collecting. Visually, the brands of video game companies were compared and analysed. The challenge of the brand was to create something that was video game themed, yet didn't feel juvenile.
